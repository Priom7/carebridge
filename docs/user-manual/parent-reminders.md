# Parent Medicine Reminders

## When a reminder appears

Read the medicine name, amount, food instruction, and time. Choose one large action:

- **I took it** after taking the medicine as prescribed.
- **Remind me in 15 minutes** to snooze.
- **Skip this dose** only when appropriate for the care plan.
- **I need help** to alert the caregiver immediately.

CareBridge does not tell you whether a medicine is medically appropriate. Contact the caregiver or a qualified clinician if instructions are unclear.

## Offline use

Reminder actions can be stored on the phone while offline. The app labels them as queued and will synchronize when connected. Do not repeatedly tap an action.

## Reminder history

Open the history icon to review scheduled, taken, snoozed, skipped, missed, escalated, resolved, and cancelled events. Every entry shows the care timezone.

## Make reminders more reliable

- Allow CareBridge notifications.
- Keep battery and background permissions enabled.
- Review Focus/Do Not Disturb and silent-mode settings.
- Keep the phone charged and connected when possible.

Mobile operating systems and connectivity can prevent delivery. CareBridge is not an emergency service and cannot guarantee an alarm. Call a caregiver, trusted local contact, or local emergency service when help is urgent.
