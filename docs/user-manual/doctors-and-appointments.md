# Doctors and Appointments

## Doctor directory

Open **More → Appointments → Doctors**. Add contact details, speciality, hospital, visiting hours, and notes. Doctors are linked to the selected care profile.

## Schedule and prepare

Tap **Add appointment**, select a doctor, and enter the date, time, timezone, location, reason, questions, required reports, attachments, and caregiver notes. CareBridge rejects past appointments and visits within one hour of an existing appointment for that profile.

Open an appointment to review the visit pack. It is an in-app preparation preview; PDF export is a future feature.

## Finish and follow up

After the visit, add the summary and indicate whether follow-up is required. Enter the agreed follow-up date. Completion appears in the local health timeline.
