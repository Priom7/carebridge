# Documents

## Find a document

Select the correct care profile, then open **Documents**. Search by title, doctor, or tag; filter by document type; optionally include archived records.

## Upload

Tap **Upload document**, choose a PDF/JPG/PNG or take a photo, and complete its metadata. Files must be no larger than 20 MB. Select visibility explicitly—emergency-contact access never implies document access.

## View and manage

Open a document for its protected local preview, metadata, links, and access history. Download/share actions verify and record access. Archive hides a record from the normal vault; delete removes access and adds an audit event.

The viewer covers sensitive content whenever CareBridge becomes inactive. Device screenshots cannot be prevented on every platform, so avoid opening health documents while screen sharing.
